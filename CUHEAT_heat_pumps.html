Did you know that the average household loses 5-30% of their energy bill on inefficient home heating?

In the North Country, that can add up to $80-$480 a year!

Not to mention that inefficient heating wastes resources and adds more
greenhouse gases into our atmosphere.

No matter what kind of house you have, you can improve your home energy
efficiency by adapting to heat pump systems!

Heat pumps can supply cold air in the summer and warm air in the winter.

Three main types of heat pumps:


    Air Source Heat Pump


    Water Source Heat Pump


    Ground Source (Geothermal) Heat Pump
