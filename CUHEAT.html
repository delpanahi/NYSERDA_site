Home Energy Survey

CUHEAT is a partnership between Clarkson University students & faculty and the New York State Energy Research and Development Authority (NYSERDA).

We are asking homeowners to share information about their current home energy heating systems.

This information will allow us to determine which homes can be granted aid from NYSERDA to adapt affordable clean energy systems in their homes.
